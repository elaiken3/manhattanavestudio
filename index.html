06

Common Questions.

How much does a custom website cost?

Custom web development starts at $750. Final pricing depends on scope — number of pages, integrations, and design complexity. Every quote is fixed, not hourly, so you know the total before we start.

How long does a typical project take?

Most small-business sites launch in 3–5 weeks from discovery to go-live. Larger multi-page or e-commerce projects run 6–10 weeks. We give a firm timeline after the discovery call.

Do you work with clients outside Brooklyn?

Yes. We're Brooklyn-rooted but work with clients across NYC and the United States. Discovery and reviews happen over video calls; everything else is shared via live preview URLs.

What's included in the SEO foundations?

Every site ships with semantic HTML, meta tags, Open Graph and Twitter cards, JSON-LD schema markup, a sitemap.xml, robots.txt, Google Search Console connection, and GA4 with conversion event tracking — no upsell required.

Do I own the code?

Yes, completely. You own the code, the design files, the domain, and the hosting account. Nothing is locked inside a proprietary builder. If you ever want to move it, you can.

What's the difference between the care plans?

Maintenance ($150/mo) covers updates, monitoring, and 1 hour of support. Growth ($300/mo) adds monthly analytics, Search Console review, and SEO recommendations. Partner ($500/mo) adds ad pixel and email management for clients running active marketing.
