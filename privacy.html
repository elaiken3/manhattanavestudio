Privacy Policy

Manhattan Ave Studio ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you visit manhattanavestudio.com, why we collect it, and how to contact us with questions.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact form submissions. When you submit our contact form we receive your name, email address, project type, and message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry.
  • Analytics. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site — pages viewed, approximate location, device type, referrer, and anonymized engagement events (clicks, form starts, scroll depth). IP addresses are anonymized.
  • Server logs. Our host (Vercel) may log standard request data such as IP address, user agent, and timestamps for security and reliability.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to inquiries and deliver requested services.
  • To improve site content and performance.
  • To meet legal and security obligations.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your information. We share it only with:

  • Resend — to deliver contact-form emails to us.
  • Google Analytics — for aggregated traffic analysis.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider.

4. Cookies

We use only the cookies set by Google Analytics for traffic measurement. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by enabling "Do Not Track" / similar privacy settings in your browser.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by emailing hello@manhattanavestudio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes

If we update this policy we will revise the date above. Material changes will be posted on this page.
